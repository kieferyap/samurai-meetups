【TOUR REPORT：The 3rd Discovery Tour ―Asakusa&Hanami 浅草&花見―】
　
We held a Hanami(Cherry blossum viewing) Party at Ueno Park, one of Japan's most crowded, noisy and popular spots for cherry blossom parties. There features more than 1000 trees along the street leading towards the National Museum and around Shinobazu Pond. We walked around Asakusa town to buy some foods&drinkset's before and had a great Hanami party :)
